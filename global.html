Some useful tricks when entering a new market

1 Come equipped!
  • Enter the new market with a PRO sales team on board.
  • Know your competitors and laser-focus on the most promising target verticals.
  • Outsource the routine and focus on 1) sales and 2) product / service market fit.
2 Be a local resident
  • Hire local (born and raised) AEs to help you get a better footing.
  • Localize your sales collateral, pricing plans, and killer features.
  • Expand your LI & FB network across industry experts and your prospects.
